Which Is Right for You?

Choose implants for missing teeth or long-term solutions. Choose veneers for cosmetic smile improvements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which lasts longer?
Implants last 20-30+ years. Veneers last 10-15 years. Implants are permanent; veneers need replacement.
Can I get both?
Yes. Use implants for missing teeth and veneers on remaining teeth for a cohesive smile.
Which is better for smile makeovers?
For cosmetics: veneers. For missing teeth: implants. For full smile redesign: both combined.
Do they feel natural?
Both feel natural when done well. Implants function like real teeth; veneers are purely cosmetic.
