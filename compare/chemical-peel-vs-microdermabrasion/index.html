Which Is Right for You?

Choose chemical peels for dramatic results and don't mind downtime. Choose microdermabrasion for minimal downtime and gentle maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which has more downtime?
Chemical peels require 1-2 weeks visible recovery. Microdermabrasion has minimal downtime (redness only).
Can I combine them?
Yes. Light microdermabrasion before a peel can enhance penetration.
Which is better for dark skin?
Microdermabrasion is safer. Chemical peels (especially stronger ones) risk pigmentation issues on dark skin.
How long until results appear?
Chemical peels: 2-3 weeks after peeling. Microdermabrasion: gradual over 4-6 sessions.
