Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I start receiving leads?
Most providers receive their first lead within 3–7 days of activation. High-demand specialties like med spa, dentist, and botox typically see leads within 24–48 hours.
What information comes with each lead?
Every lead includes the patient's name, phone number, email address, the service they're looking for, and their city. You can contact them directly via phone or email.
Are there any setup fees or contracts?
No setup fees, no contracts. Pay-per-lead plans are charged only when we deliver a lead. Monthly plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment — cancel any time.
What cities and specialties do you cover?
We currently cover 50+ US cities including New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, San Diego, Austin, and more — across 17 medical and aesthetic specialties.
What if a lead is invalid or not qualified?
We stand behind our lead quality. If a lead has invalid contact information or doesn't match your specialty, contact us within 48 hours and we'll issue a credit or replacement.
Can I get exclusive leads for my city?
Yes — our Exclusive Territory plan gives you first-access to all leads in your specialty within your city. Slots are limited to one provider per specialty per city. Inquire early to lock in your market.
