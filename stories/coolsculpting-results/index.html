What Is CoolSculpting?

  • Non-invasive fat reduction using cold technology
  • FDA-cleared for reducing stubborn fat pockets
  • Targets abdomen, flanks, thighs, double chin

How Results Develop

  • Fat cells freeze at higher temps than skin cells
  • Frozen cells die and are naturally removed
  • Body gradually eliminates dead fat over weeks

Timeline to Results

  • Weeks 1-3: Initial changes begin
  • Weeks 3-8: Progressive improvement continues
  • Weeks 8-12: Full results typically visible

Expected Reduction

  • Average 20-25% fat reduction per treatment area
  • Multiple sessions for more dramatic results
  • Results maintain with healthy lifestyle

No Downtime

  • Return to normal activities immediately
  • No surgery, no anesthesia required
  • Minimal discomfort during procedure

Best Candidates

  • Good candidates already near target weight
  • Works for stubborn fat resistant to diet/exercise
  • Not a weight loss solution for obesity
