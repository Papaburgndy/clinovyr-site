What Are Lip Fillers?

  • Injectable gels that add volume to lips
  • Most common: hyaluronic acid-based
  • Enhances lip shape, fullness, and definition

Popular Filler Types

  • Juvederm: Smooth gel, natural results
  • Restylane: Particle-based, more structure
  • Belotero: Ultra-smooth for fine lines

The Procedure

  • Quick 15-30 minute appointment
  • Topical numbing applied first
  • Filler injected strategically into lips

Results Timeline

  • Immediate: Initial fullness visible
  • Day 3-5: Swelling reduces, final shape emerges
  • Optimal results: Weeks 1-2

How Long Results Last

  • Typically 6-12 months depending on type
  • Body naturally metabolizes filler
  • Touch-ups needed to maintain results

Costs & Considerations

  • Average cost: $500-1000 per syringe
  • Most people need 0.5-1.5 syringes
  • Total: $300-1500 per treatment session
