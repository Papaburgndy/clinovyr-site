What Is Hormone Therapy?

  • Treatment using hormones to manage symptoms
  • Addresses hormone imbalances
  • Used for menopause, andropause, thyroid issues

Types of Hormone Therapy

  • Estrogen/Progesterone for menopause
  • Testosterone for low T in men
  • Thyroid hormones for hypothyroidism

Common Applications

  • Menopausal hot flashes and night sweats
  • Mood swings and energy decline
  • Sexual dysfunction and libido changes

Benefits & Risks

  • Symptom relief and quality of life improvement
  • Risk-benefit varies by individual health
  • Requires ongoing monitoring and adjustment

Hormone Testing

  • Blood tests measure hormone levels
  • Thyroid panel, testosterone, estrogen levels
  • Baseline testing guides treatment decisions

Is It Right for You?

  • Consultation with specialist required
  • Medical history and symptoms reviewed
  • Personalized treatment plan created
