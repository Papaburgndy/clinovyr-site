Check Credentials

  • Verify DDS or DMD degree from accredited school
  • Confirm current dental license with state board
  • Look for specialty certifications if needed

Read Patient Reviews

  • Check Google, Yelp, Healthgrades ratings
  • Look for patterns in feedback
  • Note how dentist responds to complaints

Consider Experience

  • Years in practice and reputation
  • Specialized training in your needed services
  • Modern technology and techniques

Evaluate Office Environment

  • Clean, modern, well-maintained facility
  • Friendly, professional staff
  • Open communication about procedures

Insurance & Costs

  • In-network with your dental insurance
  • Clear pricing structure provided
  • Flexible payment options available

Schedule Consultation

  • Meet dentist before committing
  • Discuss your dental goals and concerns
  • Get treatment plan and cost estimates
